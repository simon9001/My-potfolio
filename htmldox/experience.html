My Experience at KeRRA

Real-world impact through technology at Kenya Rural Roads Authority – Murang’a Region.

I served as an IT Specialist at the Kenya Rural Roads Authority (KeRRA) – Murang’a Region, where I was part of a technical team dedicated to supporting road infrastructure through efficient IT services.


My responsibilities ranged from system and network administration to end-user support, ensuring seamless operation of both field and office systems. I implemented solutions that improved data accuracy, security, and availability for engineering and administrative teams.


Key Roles & Skills Applied:

  • System Administration and Configuration
  • ICT User Support and Troubleshooting
  • Computer Networking Setup & Maintenance
  • Software Installation and Maintenance
  • Database Management and Backup
  • Technical Documentation and Reporting


This hands-on role refined my ability to solve real-world problems with speed and precision. It also gave me confidence working in government systems and strengthened my drive to build IT solutions that serve communities.
